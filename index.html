I used Node.js when I started. I also tried to get into frontend development, but when I saw people creating new frameworks every month, it became hard to keep up. People would start using one framework, calling it “all-in-one,” then switch to another, saying “this is the one.” I left frontend development after that.

I then focused on backend development and grew interested in it. I tried learning lower-level languages like C, Rust, and Go.
