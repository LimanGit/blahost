How it works

Step 1

Install on your devices

Download Blahost on any device you own, such as a laptop, desktop, smart TV, or spare phone. The more devices you connect, the more you can earn.

Step 2

Let it run in the background

Blahost only uses resources your devices are not already using. It monitors your activity and backs off whenever you need full performance.

Step 3

Get paid monthly

Earnings are tracked in real time on your dashboard. You can withdraw once a month to your bank account or a supported payment method.

What you share

Bandwidth

A portion of your unused internet connection. Blahost sets a limit so your own browsing and streaming are never affected.

Idle compute

Light processing tasks that run only when your device is plugged in and the screen is off or idle. It does not drain battery on laptops or phones.

Storage (optional)

If you have free disk space you want to contribute, you can opt in. This is not required and can be turned off at any time.

Nothing else

Blahost does not access your files, messages, browsing history, or any personal data. What you share is limited to the three categories above.

How much can I earn?

Earnings depend on how many devices you connect, how long they stay online, and your internet speed. A single always-on desktop with a decent connection typically earns between $5 and $20 a month. Households with several devices running around the clock can earn more.

You can see an estimate before you commit. After installing, Blahost will show you a monthly earnings projection based on your setup before you flip the switch.

Common questions

Is this safe to run on my home network?

Yes. Blahost does not expose your local network to outside traffic. All outbound connections are encrypted and only go to Blahost servers.

Will it slow down my internet or computer?

Not noticeably. Blahost has built-in limits that cap how much bandwidth and CPU it uses. If you open a video call or start a large download, it backs off automatically.

Can I choose which devices to include?

Yes. You manage each device separately from your dashboard. You can pause or remove any device at any time.

How do I get paid?

You can withdraw earnings once they pass a $5 minimum. Supported options include direct bank transfer and PayPal. More options are coming.

What happens if I uninstall it?

Any pending earnings are still paid out at the next payment cycle. Blahost leaves no background processes behind after uninstall.
